About the Podcast

This podcast was inspired by the conversations David had with artists while waiting on a render to complete on the farm. David looked forward to these spontaneous chats each day as these coworkers quickly became long lasting friends. These talks soon expanded to coordinators, producers, supervisors and managers as they sought a quiet place to take a breather. David's office was quickly dubbed Café Prodman and became the go to office for good vibes and nice conversation (and occasionally resource and studio management).

With this podcast, David aims to share the stories of the incredible people he's worked with during his VFX career so far. His hope is that someone will listen and be inspired to contribute amazing work to the visual effects industry.

David Hirsh

Host, Producer, & VFX Coordinator


David's IMDb

Born in South America, David Hirsh grew up in Virginia and attended East Tennessee State University. In college, David spent most of his time out of class learning new software and making side projects. Although this hampered his college social life, it prepared him to enter the VFX industry as an IT technician at FuseFX. In the 4 years since he entered the VFX industy, David has climbed to the role of 3D Production Manager at Crafty Apes VFX.
