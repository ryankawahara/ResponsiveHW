Wait, what even is VFX?



VFX stands for

visual effects

.

Unlike special effects, which happen on set,

visual effects

enhance the story of a film, TV show, or music video in post-production.



Whether it's explosions,

explosions,

spaceships, greensceens, or motion capture, Hollywood's

visual effects

industry works tirelessly to bring fantastical ideas to life.
