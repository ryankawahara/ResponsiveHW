Oscar Velasquez is a creative producer whose VFX work includes Thor, Lil Dicky's "Pillow Talking," and the Hamilton Mixtapes' "Immigrants (We Get the Job Done)."

In this episode, David and Oscar chat about community service, how Oscar got his start, and also shout out a lot of people who helped him along the way.
